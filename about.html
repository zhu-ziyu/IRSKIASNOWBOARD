Iroquois Ridge High School Ski & Snowboard Team

Welcome to the IRHS Ski & Snowboard Team! We are proud athletes from Iroquois Ridge High School who chase snowy thrills every winter. Under expert coaching, we train on alpine Cross courses and varied terrain. mastering both classic alpine skiing and freestyle snowboarding. United by grit, sportsmanship, and a passion for speed, we compete in regional and school-level races, striving to push our limits and bring honor to IRHS.
